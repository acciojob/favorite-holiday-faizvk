Paris, France


Paris, often called the "City of Light", is a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its picturesque streets, world-famous landmarks, and charming cafes make it a dream destination for travelers.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris offering breathtaking views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the Mona Lisa and countless art treasures.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre – A vibrant neighborhood known for its bohemian charm.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A romantic journey through historic Paris.
  • Croissant – A flaky, buttery pastry perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate French sweets.
  • Escargot – A traditional delicacy cooked with garlic and herbs.
  • Ratatouille – A hearty dish of stewed vegetables.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes served with sweet or savory fillings.
